# DeployShield : Zero-Downtime Deployment Automation for AWS Startups

## Business Problem Statement
### Who is the company?
TezCart ek Bangalore-based D2C fashion startup hai jo ₹8L/month GMV generate karta hai aur sirf ek AWS EC2 instance par production run kar raha hai.

### What exact pain are they facing?
Har deployment manually SSH se hota hai, jisse week me 3–4 production outages aur lagbhag ₹30,600/month revenue loss ho raha hai.

### What are the constraints?
3-member development team, zero DevOps engineer, AWS Free Tier budget aur 6 weeks baad Diwali sale jahan traffic aur revenue dono dramatically increase honge.

### What happens if they don't solve it?
Diwali sale ke dauran downtime se ₹50L+ GMV risk par aa sakta hai, developer burnout badhega aur future investors unke deployment process ko major red flag maan sakte hain.
